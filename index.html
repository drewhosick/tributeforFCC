MY TRIBUTE TO JACQUES VILLENEUVE

Legends Of Motorsports, CC BY-SA 2.0 https://creativecommons.org/licenses/by-sa/2.0, via Wikimedia Commons

Jacques Villeneuve is a Canadian racing driver. He's won many races and championships including the 1997 Formula One World Championship and the 1995 PPG Indy Car World Series. He also won the 1995 Indianapolis 500. Villeneuve has also raced in Nascar, touring car and races like the 1000km of Spa.

More recently, Jacques Villeneuve has been racing in the 2021 Nascar Whelen Euro Series. He's raced in this series in 2021.

He is the son of former racer Gilles Villeneuve and the nephew of Jacques Villeneuve Sr who is a snowmobile racer.

On top of all these accolades, Jacques also launched a music career and has released music.
